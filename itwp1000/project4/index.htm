Tower Of God Synopsis

Adventure

The story is centered around a boy named Twenty-Fifth Baam who chases Rachel his childhood friend who wants to climb to the top of the mysterious Tower.

Action

When the boy enters the tower looking for his childhood friend Rachel, he is told by a rabbit that he would need to climb the tower to do so. And subsequently made to battle a giant snake-like Eel called the White Steel Eel to climb the tower.

Fantasy

The Tower" is a mysterious structure that is completely enclosed and hosts many unique environments. It is permeated by an element called "Shinsu", which has strange properties similar to magic in other comic universes

Drama

In the end, when he finds her and wants to climb the tower with her, but she betrays him and tries to kill him.
