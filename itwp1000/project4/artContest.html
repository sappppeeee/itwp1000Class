Welcome to the Art Gallery Contest of the Tower of God Art club. Every year, we host a fan art contest for the community of Tower of God. Where the fan can design or draw a character to the webcomic. Well, it doesn't matter what you chose to draw but it must meet the Contest Guidelines to enter the Contest. But the winner of the contest is decided by a survey that you can take.
